Gujarati kadhi

Rooted in tradition, prepared by the next generation

Dish photo by Dheerajk88 (Wikimedia Commons)

Ingredients:

  • Plain yogurt (dahi)
  • Water
  • Besan (gram flour)
  • Salt
  • Turmeric powder
  • Grated ginger
  • Green chili (finely chopped)
  • Ghee or oil
  • Mustard seeds
  • Fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • Asafoetida (hing)
  • Small cinnamon piece
  • Fresh coriander (chopped)

Steps:

  1. Whisk 1 cup yogurt, 2 tbsp besan, and 2½ cups water until smooth
  2. Add ¼ tsp turmeric, 1–2 tbsp sugar or jaggery, salt, 1 tsp grated ginger, and 1 chopped green chili; mix well
  3. Pour into a pot and cook on medium, stirring continuously until it gently boils
  4. Lower heat and simmer 10–12 minutes until slightly thick and smooth
  5. In a small pan, heat 1–2 tbsp ghee or oil
  6. Add ½ tsp mustard seeds (let pop), ½ tsp cumin seeds, 4–5 methi seeds, 1 dry red chili, pinch hing, and 8–10 curry leaves; cook 15–20 seconds
  7. Pour tempering into kadhi
  8. Simmer 2 minutes, turn off heat, add chopped coriander, rest 5 minutes, serve
